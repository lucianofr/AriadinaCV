## Quick reference

- **Brand:** Ariadina Astori Porto · Consultoria em Gestão de Projetos · BID
- **Voice:** sóbria, técnica, conclusiva. Portuguese (BR) default. No emoji, no exclamations.
- **Colors:** Navy `#0D2747` (primary) · Cyan `#3FB8D6` (accent) · Blue `#2563EB` (action/CTA) · White canvas · Slate neutrals.
- **Type:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` everywhere (display + UI + body). `JetBrains Mono` for data.
- **Icons:** Lucide @ stroke 1.5.
- **Radii:** 6 / 10 / 16 + pill. More generous than classical-institutional.
- **Motion:** minimal, 260–400ms, ease-out, no bounce.
- **Tokens file:** `colors_and_type.css` (import this first).
- **Mark:** globe/network with 3 cyan dots (`assets/logo-mark.svg`).

## When in doubt

- Prefer fewer, larger elements with breathing room over dense grids.
- Quantify ("R$ 612 mi", "42 indicadores", "−18%") rather than describing.
- Use third-person impersonal for reports; controlled first-person for portfolio.
- No emoji, no decorative SVG illustrations of people. Use Lucide icons functionally.
- No serif fonts. No brass/gold. No warm tones.
